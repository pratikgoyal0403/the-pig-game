rules of the game.
1. Two players can play game at a time.
2. A player can roll a dice any number of time until the player opt to hold or gets 1 on dice
3. When a player opt to hold his current score is added to overall score.
4. If a player gets 1 on dice his current score becomes 0 and next player rolls the dice.
5. The player with 100 points first wins the game.
